The Founder of Nepal and the History of Nepal

Nepal was founded on 25th of September in 1768, Prithvi Narayan Shah was crowned King as he was the founder of Nepal.
Before Nepal was named Nepal it was called Gorkha Kingdom which was ruled by Prithvi Narayan Shah from 1768 to 1775. Prithvi Narayan Shah was called the Father of Nepal as he started unification of Nepal.
Nepal has never been conquered because Nepal had strong rulers that protected Nepal very well.
The Rana (King) of Nepal made an alliance with the British Empire so that is why Nepal was never in the British Empire as the other parts of South Asia were conquered by the British Empire.
Nepal served as a buffer state between imperial China and British India.
Many many years later on the 1st of June 2001 the royal family of Nepal was massacred, 10 people died including the killer who allegedly was the Crown Prince Dipendra Bir Bikram Shah and 5 were injured but there are still people from the royal family who are still alive but on the 28th of May 2008 was when Nepal became a democracy country making 240 years of monarchy to an end but a lot of people miss the royal family of Nepal that is why a wave of people want the royal family again so they want the former King of Nepal Raja(king) Gyanendra and his family to rule again.
The royal place is a museum now which people pay money which is Nepali Rupees to see inside.

Nepal Culture and The Language

The Culture of Nepal has the most beautiful cultures as there are mostly Hindus and Buddhist that have homes in Nepal.
Nepal has 142 ethnic groups which are Newar, Bahun, Gurung, Magar, Tharu, Sherpa etc, who speak multiple languages and have different traditions and cultures which is why Nepal is known as a diverse country as people from different backgrounds that have different traditions, culture and festivals live in Nepal.
Nepal has a goddess called the Kumari Devi which is the living goddess so a little girl is chosen to be the Kumari Devi and after she hits menstruation another girl is chosen.
The Language of Nepal is called Nepali it is an Indo-Aryan language native to the Himalayas in South Asia.
Some parts of India speak Nepali such as Sikkim, West Bengal and Darjeeling but it is mostly Nepal who speaks Nepali as it is the official language of Nepal.

8 Mountain higher than 8,000 metres located in Nepal

There are 14 mountains that are in Asia that are higher than 8,000 metres but most of them are in Nepal and Mt Everest is the tallest mountain out of them. Mt Everest is about 8,849 metres high up.
Sir Edmund Hillary and Tenzing Norgay were the first people ever who climbed up Mt Everest and went to the summit area.
It takes about 6 to 9 weeks to climb up Mt Everest. Mt Everest was known as Peak XV but it was renamed for Sir George Everest.
The first person who died on Mt Everest was George Mallory.
